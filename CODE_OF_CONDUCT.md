# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Project participants are expected to communicate respectfully, give actionable technical feedback, acknowledge uncertainty, and protect private or proprietary information.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, deliberate disclosure of private information, fabricated benchmark claims, or attempts to introduce proprietary data into public fixtures.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner. Reports will be reviewed fairly and handled with appropriate confidentiality. Maintainers may edit, reject, or remove contributions and restrict participation when necessary to protect the community and project integrity.
